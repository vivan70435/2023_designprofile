理念 CONCEPT

點擊前往 | Link：新朋派實業 Yamicook

新朋派實業為一個推廣飲食文化、烹飪教育及社會公益的平台。

在此專案中，負責範圍有：視覺設計、介面設計、動態切版，取用商標中的標準色，去和美食視覺做搭配，並以簡潔的編排讓大量資訊呈現的清楚明瞭。

Yamicook is an organization dedicated to promoting culinary culture, cooking education, and social welfare.

In this project, my responsibilities included visual design, interface design, and dynamic layout. I utilized the standard colors from the brand logo and combined them with the visual elements of food. The design aimed for simplicity and clear presentation to effectively communicate a large amount of information.
