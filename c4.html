543 COFFEE / 伍肆參咖啡

YEAR / 2019CLIENT / 543 COFFEEROLE / CIS設計

理念 CONCEPT

和朋友一起喝咖啡聊伍肆參是最好的精神糧食，調劑了你我疲憊的身心。 因應客戶希望中文標準字能有別於單一中文的需求，所以將數字諧音隱藏在中文筆畫的字裡行間，增添玩味。

主色調以淺卡其色近金色為基底，再搭配米色為輔色，來提升整體質感。圖案設計則是咖啡豆和對話框的意象做結合，形成了咖啡散發出的香氣。讓單一符號能有三層含義，不會過於單薄。

Having coffee and chatting with friends is the best source of mental nourishment, rejuvenating our tired minds and bodies. In response to the client's request for a distinctive Chinese standard font that goes beyond conventional Chinese characters, we have hidden the phonetic meanings within the strokes of the characters, adding an element of playfulness.

The main color theme is a light khaki tone with a touch of gold, complemented by a beige accent to enhance the overall texture. The design incorporates the imagery of coffee beans and speech bubbles, symbolizing the aroma emitted by coffee. This allows a single symbol to carry three layers of meaning, ensuring it is not overly simplistic.
