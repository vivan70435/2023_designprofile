MAMA BAKERY / 麻麻烘焙坊

YEAR / 2019CLIENT / MAMA BAKERYROLE / CIS設計

理念 CONCEPT

麻麻(目前更名為一天一天)一間位於彰化的新創甜點店，主要研發用食物的原味做出美味的甜點， 讓大家吃的安心又開心！

以業主的形象繪製的頭像，並且配戴招牌山巒吐司意向而設計的糕點師帽組成主要肖像，並搭配專屬設計的字體，選色方面，則是取自焦香意象的褐色（PANTONE P 16-16 U）以及白糖意象的白色（PANTONE P 1-1 U），希望能將嗅覺也放入視覺設計中，整體營造出日系、溫潤的烘焙職人感覺。

Mama Bakery (currently renamed as "One Day One Day") is a new dessert shop located in Changhua. They specialize in creating delicious desserts using the natural flavors of food, aiming to provide a safe and joyful dining experience for everyone.

The logo design features a customized portrait of the owner, wearing the signature Mountain Loaf Toast hat that represents the shop. The portrait is complemented by a specially designed font. In terms of colors, the design incorporates a rich brown color (PANTONE P 16-16 U) representing the aroma of roasted ingredients, along with a white color (PANTONE P 1-1 U) symbolizing the sweetness of sugar. The intention is to incorporate the sense of smell into visual design, creating an overall Japanese-inspired and warm artisanal baking atmosphere.

PROJECT DETAIL

LOGO細部 ｜（1）以招牌山巒吐司為意象設計的糕點師帽。 （2）叉子意象。
These elements, the Mountain Loaf Toast-inspired pastry chef hat and the fork, are integrated into the logo design to represent the essence of the bakery's offerings and create a visually appealing and memorable logo.

標準色選定為：具有焦香意象的褐色、具有白糖意象的白色。 ｜ 字體皆選定較圓滑的字型，以符合品牌定位。
The chosen color scheme for the brand is a rich brown color that represents the aroma and warmth of toasted flavors, and a white color that symbolizes the essence of white sugar. These colors are selected to evoke a sense of deliciousness and sweetness.
For the fonts, rounded and smooth typefaces are chosen to align with the brand positioning. These typefaces convey a friendly and approachable vibe, enhancing the overall branding of the bakery.
