HANG DOU / 夯豆咖啡

YEAR / 2020CLIENT / HANG DOU COFFEEROLE / CIS設計

理念 CONCEPT

夯豆咖啡，位於台中北屯區的新創咖啡廳，意在讓生活的回甘比咖啡更簡單 ，咖啡給人的第一印象是苦味，然而細品後是酸、是甜，而最後回甘的尾韻則是讓人著迷的關鍵。

在logo設計上，因應客戶指定的手寫字風格，所以在多方筆畫風格嘗試後，選定了此款帶有似鋼筆毛邊的筆刷風格，並以思源宋體去做英文名字的設計，在選色上因搭配業主店內之後的北歐工業風格裝修，所以選用了金色（Pantone 146 UP ）為logo主色，以達到在深色空間中能跳脫的目的。輔色則選用業主喜好的符合北歐工業風格的靛青色（Pantone 5395 UP ）。

夯豆咖啡 is a new coffee shop located in the Beitun District of Taichung, Taiwan. The aim of the cafe is to make life's sweetness simpler than coffee itself. While coffee is often associated with bitterness, upon closer examination, it reveals notes of acidity and sweetness, leaving a lingering and enchanting aftertaste.

For the logo design, the client requested a handwritten style. After experimenting with various brushstroke styles, a brush font with a slightly rough and textured edge was chosen. The English name of the cafe was designed using the Source Han Serif font. In terms of color selection, the golden color (Pantone 146 UP) was chosen as the main color for the logo to complement the cafe's interior, which features a Nordic industrial style. This choice allows the logo to stand out in darker spaces. The secondary color selected is a deep indigo blue (Pantone 5395 UP), which aligns with the owner's preference and the overall Nordic industrial aesthetic.

PROJECT DETAIL

LOGO細部

標準字｜主標在中英皆採用有襯線的字型，營造文藝質感；內文則是無襯線的細黑體，讓訊息量多時，不易有擁擠感。
For the main title, both Chinese and English texts use serif fonts to create a literary and artistic feel. The choice of serif fonts adds elegance and sophistication to the design. On the other hand, for the body text, a sans-serif font is used. This helps to maintain legibility and prevents the text from appearing crowded, especially when conveying a larger amount of information. The use of a clean and minimalist sans-serif font ensures clarity and readability in the body text.
