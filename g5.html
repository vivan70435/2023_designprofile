YEAR / 2020CLIENT / MAMA BAKERYROLE / 插畫繪製/排版文宣

理念 CONCEPT

KNOCK KNOCK!為此萬聖節活動的主題，是不給糖就搗蛋時敲門的聲響，所以在標準字設計上，將KNOCK的O轉換為門的意象，第一次敲門時；門為緊閉的狀態，第二次敲門時；門打開可以看見此次主打的甜點裝扮成幽靈的應景打扮，上面是馬林糖、下面是磅蛋糕。

在繪製風格上，是較為美式漫畫的風格。角色塑造，則是將甜品們披上床單，佯裝成幽靈的樣子，除了是為了符合萬聖節裝扮外，另一點是在活動開始前，並未直接向消費者透露此次會銷售哪些口味，所以用床單包覆的話，也可以營造出含有驚喜的懸念。

KNOCK KNOCK! was the theme for this Halloween event, inspired by the tradition of trick-or-treating and the sound of knocking on doors. In the typography design, the "O" in "KNOCK" was transformed into a door symbol. During the first knock, the door appears closed, and during the second knock, the door opens to reveal the featured desserts dressed up as ghosts for the occasion. On top, there is a marshmallow, and below, there is a pound cake.

The drawing style adopted a more American comic book style. The characters were depicted as desserts wearing bed sheets, pretending to be ghosts. This not only aligned with the Halloween theme but also created an element of surprise. Before the event started, the specific flavors of the desserts were not disclosed to the consumers, so the use of bed sheets added an air of anticipation and mystery.

PROJECT DETAIL

標準字細部｜第一次敲門時；門為緊閉的狀態，第二次敲門時；門打開可以看見此次主打的甜點裝扮成幽靈的應景打扮。
In the typography design, during the first knock, the door is depicted as tightly closed. The letter "O" in "KNOCK" represents the door and is shown as a closed door symbol. This conveys the anticipation and suspense of the trick-or-treating tradition. During the second knock, the door opens, revealing the main attraction of the event: desserts dressed up as ghosts. The door swings open, and the desserts are showcased in their ghostly attire. This visual transformation adds a playful and festive element to the design, capturing the Halloween spirit.

標準色選定｜以此次主打口味南瓜、紫薯、抹茶去做色彩搭配。
The color palette is chosen based on the main flavors of the event: pumpkin, purple sweet potato, and matcha. These colors are incorporated into the design to create a harmonious and visually appealing composition.
