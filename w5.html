GIFT TO GROW（台譯：成長集點送 / 港譯：成長積分賞）是幫寶適建置的一個常設積分活動，線下掃描正品的QRCode後，即可線上填寫資料取得免費試用包（彼時活動詳情參考）。台灣以及香港版本不同之處，在於台灣多數使用者是使用LINE此款通訊軟體，所以將入口頁和官方LINE做串連；香港版本則是獨立建置的手機版網頁。

在此專案中，負責所有視覺設計以及介面設計，因港台活動操作不同，所以在介面流程設計上，感謝團隊的前端工程師以及企劃的協助，才能讓介面在使用流程上更加順暢。主視覺是將官方吉祥物奠定在遊行同樂的情境下，所以幫其繪製同“拉拉褲”系列的代表色紫色的軍服，以讓吉祥物能從大面積的冷色調背景色中跳脫，整體視覺是嬰幼兒用品粉嫩且柔和的視覺意象。

GIFT TO GROW is a permanent points accumulation program established by Pampers. During the offline event, participants can scan the QR code of genuine products and fill in their information online to receive free trial packages (refer to the event details at that time). The main difference between the Taiwan and Hong Kong versions lies in the user behavior, as most users in Taiwan use the messaging app LINE. Therefore, the entry page and the official LINE account were connected in the Taiwan version, while the Hong Kong version was built as a standalone mobile web page.

In this project, I was responsible for all visual and interface designs. Due to the different operations for the Taiwan and Hong Kong events, I would like to express my gratitude to the team's frontend engineers and planners for their assistance in designing a smoother user flow. The main visual design depicted the official mascot in a festive parade setting, dressed in a military uniform in the representative color of the "拉拉褲" (Pull-Up Pants) series, which allowed the mascot to stand out from the large areas of cool-toned background colors. The overall visual presentation conveyed a soft and gentle visual image, consistent with the pink and tender imagery of baby products.
