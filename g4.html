PAMPERS / 幫寶適社群視覺

YEAR / 2020CLIENT / 幫寶適嬰兒紙尿布ROLE / 社群視覺

理念 CONCEPT

協助幫寶適社群所設計貼文視覺，並部分含有基本動態效果。（上圖）為新年時所採用的視覺。

每次製圖時都非常有趣，因為客戶給予蠻大的彈性空間，因應不同主題，在風格上做了不同嘗試，有像PURE系列的那種輕柔的水彩風格、新年時的中國風、成長積分賞的卡通風...萬變不離其宗的是要讓各式風格能依舊有幫寶適的基調，已不至於整體風格跑偏。

I assisted in designing visual posts for Pampers' social media platforms, incorporating basic dynamic effects. The provided image showcases the visual used during the New Year period.

It was always an exciting experience to create these graphics because the client provided a good degree of flexibility. Depending on the theme, we experimented with different styles, such as the gentle watercolor style seen in the PURE series, the Chinese-inspired design for the New Year, and the cartoonish style for the GIFT TO GROW campaign. The key was to ensure that each style maintained the essence of Pampers' branding, so that the overall aesthetic remained consistent.
